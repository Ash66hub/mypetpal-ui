About MyPetPal

MyPetPal is a cozy virtual pet game where you raise your companion, personalize your room with decor, and meet other pet pals.

What You Can Do

  • Walk your pet and grow with Experience and levels.
  • Build and decorate your own space with themed items.
  • Connect with friends, visit their rooms, and invite them over for multiplayer parties.
  • Track progress with a global leaderboardand customize your player profile.

Built With Care

MyPetPal is actively developed and maintained with a focus on a friendly, low-friction experience across desktop and mobile.
