This Privacy Policy explains how MyPetPal collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the application and related services.

Information We Collect

  • Account details such as username, email address, and authentication provider.
  • Profile data such as profile picture and pet customization details.
  • Gameplay data such as progression, level, achievements, and decor placement.
  • Social data such as friend requests, friend lists, and in-game interactions.
  • Technical data such as browser type, device information, and basic usage logs.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To provide game functionality and save your progress.
  • To support social features, including friend connections and shared experiences.
  • To improve performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • To communicate important account, security, or service updates like password recovery.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. Data may be shared only when needed to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, protect users, or enforce platform policies.

Data Retention

We keep account and gameplay data only as long as needed to provide the service and meet legal or operational requirements. If you delete your account, associated personal data is removed or anonymized where reasonably possible.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data. No system can guarantee absolute security, but we continuously work to reduce risk.

Your Rights and Choices

  • You can review and update parts of your profile in the app.
  • You can request account deletion from profile settings.
  • You can contact us for privacy-related questions or requests.

Children's Privacy

MyPetPal is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
